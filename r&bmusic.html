Facts about R&B!

WHAT IS R&B? R&B is short for rhythm and blues. It is a kind of pop music of African-American origion with a soulful vocal style. R&B is a very popular music genre! Seven of this year's top 10 albums were R&B/Hip Hop.

How do I find R&B artists? You can download spotify, a free music app, and they have already have a public R&B playlist created. Also, you can find R&B artists on Apple Music!
